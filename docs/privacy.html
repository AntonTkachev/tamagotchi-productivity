Privacy Policy

Productivity Tamagotchi — Chrome Extension

The short version

We collect nothing. Zero. Nada. Your data never leaves your device.

What data the extension uses

Productivity Tamagotchi stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage.local API:

  • Your pet's stats (health, happiness, age, stage)
  • Your chosen pet type (rabbit, cat, dog, or parrot)
  • Your list of productive and distracting sites
  • The hostname of the current tab (used only to categorize the site)

This data is stored only in your browser and is never transmitted anywhere.

What we do NOT collect

  • No personal information
  • No browsing history
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No cookies
  • No data sent to any server

Third parties

This extension does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. It makes no network requests.

Permissions explained

  • storage — to save your pet's state and settings locally
  • alarms — to update your pet's stats every 5 minutes
  • tabs / activeTab — to read the current tab's hostname and categorize it as productive or distracting

Data deletion

You can delete all extension data at any time by clicking Reset Pet in the Settings page, or by removing the extension from Chrome.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Given the extension collects no data, we don't anticipate any changes.
